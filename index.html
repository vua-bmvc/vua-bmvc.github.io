About the Workshop Series

The Video Understanding and its Applications (VUA) Workshop series brings together researchers, practitioners, and experts in the field of computer vision and artificial intelligence to explore the latest advancements in video understanding and its real-world applications.

Our workshops cover a wide range of topics, including but not limited to action recognition, video captioning, video editing and summarization, and the application of video understanding in healthcare, media production, and security systems.
